Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Maranello Insider. We value your passion for Scuderia Ferrari and respect your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

We only collect your email address when you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter. This information is used strictly for the purpose of delivering weekly Ferrari F1 technical briefings and news.

2. Data Usage

Your email is used to send you the automated newsletter via the Resend API or SMTP. We do not sell, trade, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Unsubscription

You may unsubscribe at any time using the link provided in any of our emails or by visiting our unsubscribe page. Once you unsubscribe, your email is marked as inactive and you will no longer receive briefings.

4. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your email address. Our database is stored securely and access is restricted to the automated delivery system.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us through the official Scuderia Ferrari fan channels.
